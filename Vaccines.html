All the Information about the Vaccines shown in the table is displayed here.

Pfizer-BioNTech

















The Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine is an mRNA-based COVID-19 vaccine developed by the German company BioNTech. It is Recommended for people

12 years and older.

The Pfizer-BioNTECH Vaccine is Administered by recieving a shot in the Muscle of the upper arm. The number of shots given is two (2), each twenty-one (21) days apart.

Common Side Effects include: Pain, Redness and Swelling in the arm where u got shot, Fever, tiredness, headache, muscle pain, Chills and Nausea.



Moderna















The Moderna COVID-19 vaccine is an mRNA-based COVID-19 vaccine developed by the company ModernaTX, Inc. It is Recommended for people

aged 18 years and older.

The Moderna Vaccine is Administered by recieving a shot in the Muscle of the upper arm. The number of shots given is two (2), each twenty-eight (28) days apart.

Common Side Effects include: Pain, Redness and Swelling in the arm where u got shot, Fever, tiredness, headache, muscle pain, Chills and Nausea.


Johnson & Johnson's Janssen











The Johnson & Johnson's Janssen COVID-19 vaccine is an Viral Vector based COVID-19 vaccine manufactured by Janssen Pharmaceuticals Companies of Johnson & Johnson. It is Recommended for people

18 years and older.

The Johnson & Johnson's Janssen vaccine is Administered by recieving a shot in the Muscle of the upper arm. Only one (1) shot is given.

Common Side Effects include:Pain, Redness and Swelling in the arm where u got shot, Fever, tiredness, headache, muscle pain, Chills and Nausea.


AstraZeneca















The AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine is an Viral Vector based COVID-19 vaccine developed by Oxford University and AstraZeneca and is sold under the brand names Covishield and Vaxzevria. It is Recommended for people

aged 18 years and older.

The AstraZeneca vaccine is Administered by recieving a shot in the Muscle of the upper arm. The number of shots given is two (2), each 4-12 weeks apart.

Common Side Effects include:Injection site pain or tenderness, tiredness, headache, muscle pain, fever and chills.


Novavax















The Novavax COVID-19 vaccine is an protein subunit based COVID-19 vaccine developed by Novavax and the Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations. It is Recommended for people

aged 18 years and older.

The Novavax vaccine is Administered by recieving a shot in the Muscle of the upper arm. The number of shots given is two (2), each twenty-one (21) days apart.

Common Side Effects include: Injection site pain or tenderness, tiredness, headache, muscle pain, fatigue.
